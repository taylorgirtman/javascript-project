Mamma Mia!
Learn about pasta

By Taylor Girtman

Before your next trip to Italy or Olive Garden, learn about pasta and its countless types. Read about several types of pastas below, and click the buttons to see what they look like cooked.

Spaghetti

Spaghetti is traditionally served with a tomato marinara meatsauce. Meatballs are sometimes added on top of the pasta.

Rotini

Rotini is spiral-shaped and can include a variety of sauces like pesto or marinara. In this photo, avocado, onions and peppers are used.

Farfalle

Farfalle pastas look like little bowties. They also are often paired with marinara, pesto or cream sauces.
